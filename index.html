About Mike Kelley

I began working in an entry level computer job and was swept away with the internet boom of the mid-90’s. Along the way, I picked up advertising, budgeting and planning for large organizations. I have a unique skill set and enjoying working on innovative projects.

With over twenty years in IT, focusing primarily on the web, I bring my expertise with web design and marketing together in my current role and I am still excited by learning something new each day.
